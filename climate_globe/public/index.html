Data sources

Data sources & citations

Runtime Data (loaded by the application)

  • World country boundaries (GeoJSON) — johan/world.geo.json (GitHub maintainer: johan). Source
  • US state boundaries — PublicaMundi MappingAPI. Original source (local copy)
  • Energy Systems:
    • Share of renewables by country — Ember (2026); Energy Institute - Statistical Review of World Energy (2025) – with major processing by Our World in Data. Dataset
    • Share of electricity by source (Coal, Gas, Hydropower, Solar, Wind, Oil, Nuclear, Other renewables, Bioenergy) by country — Ember (2026); Energy Institute - Statistical Review (2025) – processed by Our World in Data. Dataset
    • Total electricity generation by country — Ember (2026); Energy Institute - Statistical Review (2025) – processed by Our World in Data. Dataset
    • Energy use per person by country — U.S. Energy Information Administration (2025); Energy Institute - Statistical Review (2025); population data (2024) – processed by Our World in Data. Dataset
    • Access to electricity by country — SDG 7.1.1, World Bank (2026) – processed by Our World in Data. Dataset
    • Cost of electricity by country (USD/kWh) — World Population Review (2026). Source page (local dataset)
    • Generation cost by source and country (USD/MWh, 2026 proxy) — OECD-NEA LCOE and IRENA 2024 costs. OECD-NEA, IRENA 2024 (dataset)
    • Battery-electric/plug-in hybrid vehicle sales share by country — International Energy Agency, Global EV Outlook 2025 – processed by Our World in Data. Dataset
    • Carbon intensity of electricity by country — Ember (2026) – processed by Our World in Data. Dataset
    • Coal units shut down by country (cumulative, 2000–2025) — Global Energy Monitor (GEM). Summary tables (local dataset)
    • Grid battery storage capacity by country (2023, top 10) — Visualized via Voronoi/Decarbonization Channel (Energy Institute). Visualization (dataset)
    • Energy trade by country (imports, exports, production, consumption; 2026 proxy for oil/gas/coal) — Dataset
  • Climate & Weather Impacts:
    • Heatwave days by country (annual mean, past 7 years, latest quarter) — Climate-Conflict Vulnerability Index (CCVI). Indicator page (local dataset)
    • Extreme weather events by country (2024 count; meteorological, hydrological, climatological disasters) — EM-DAT via HDX. Dataset page (local dataset)
    • Deaths from extreme weather by country (floods, droughts, storms, extreme temperatures) — EM-DAT/CRED – processed by Our World in Data. OWID source (dataset)
    • Annual surface temperature change by country — IMF Climate Change Indicators. Citation source (dataset)
    • Wildfire burned area by country — Global Wildfire Information System (GWIS). GWIS source (dataset)
  • Global Baseline Metrics:
    • Population by country — UN World Population Prospects (2024) – processed by Our World in Data. Dataset
    • CO₂ emissions by country (annual) — Global Carbon Budget (2025) – processed by Our World in Data. Dataset
    • CO₂ per capita by country — Our World in Data (OWID) / Global Carbon Budget. OWID source (dataset)
    • Methane emissions by country — Our World in Data (OWID). OWID source (dataset)
    • Land-use change CO₂ by country — Our World in Data (OWID). OWID source (dataset)
    • GDP by country — Feenstra et al., Penn World Table (2025) – processed by Our World in Data. Dataset
  • Climate Risk & Resilience:
    • Climate resilience snapshot by country (sea-level exposure, extreme weather deaths, cereal yield anomaly, water stress, freshwater resources, PM2.5, air-pollution mortality, ND-GAIN readiness, coal power share) — World Bank API, ND-GAIN Country Index (2026), OWID disaster data, Ember/Energy Institute. World Bank API example; ND-GAIN (local aggregated dataset)
    • Sea-level rise by country (2026 projection) — World Bank Climate Knowledge Portal (CCKP). CCKP source (dataset)
    • Climate GDP losses/benefits scenarios (Hot House, Delayed, Net Zero; 2023–2050) — IMF Climate Change Indicators (NGFS framework). IMF Climate Data (dataset)
  • Climate Policy:
    • Carbon pricing by country — World Bank Carbon Pricing Dashboard. World Bank CPD (dataset)
    • Fossil-fuel subsidies by country (2024) — IMF Working Paper 24/11. IMF WP/24/11 (dataset)
    • NDC net-zero status by country — Climate Watch. Climate Watch (dataset)
  • US State Data:
    • Existing electricity capacity by state/fuel type — U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA). Dataset
    • Historical state population by year — Josh Tauberer, historical-state-population-csv. Original source (local copy)
    • CO₂ emissions by state — U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA). Dataset
    • State GDP (SAGDP) — U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA). Dataset
    • Homeowners insurance premiums by state — Insurance Information Institute table archive (NAIC). Citation source (dataset)
    • Drought severity by state (area in D1+) — NOAA Drought Information System / U.S. Drought Monitor. Source page (dataset)
    • Wildfire burned acres by state (annual) — National Interagency Fire Center (NIFC). Source page (dataset)
  • Ocean & Cryosphere:
    • Global sea-level rise (annual) — U.S. EPA climate indicators, NOAA altimetry. EPA indicators (dataset)
    • Ocean acidity (pH scale) — U.S. EPA climate indicators. EPA (dataset)
    • Ocean heat content anomaly — U.S. EPA climate indicators. EPA (dataset)
    • Sea surface temperature anomaly — U.S. EPA climate indicators. EPA (dataset)
    • Arctic sea ice extent (annual minimum) — NSIDC Sea Ice Index. NSIDC source (dataset)
    • Antarctic sea ice extent (annual minimum) — NSIDC Sea Ice Index. NSIDC source (dataset)
  • Visualization Layers:
    • Thermal/temperature anomaly map layer — NASA GISS temperature anomaly maps. NASA GISS source (local grid dataset)

    • Generation cost (2026 proxy) methodology — Documents baseline OECD-NEA LCOE and IRENA 2024 cost-change rate adjustments. Method notes
    • Energy trade (2026 proxy) methodology — Documents compilation approach for oil/gas/coal trade flows. Method notes
    • Ocean sea-level (2026 proxy) methodology — Documents aggregation of NOAA altimetry and projection method. Method notes
