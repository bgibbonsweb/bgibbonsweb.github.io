Green Lifestyle


I work hard to live my life in the most sustainable way possible!

Zero Waste

I work really hard to make sure nothing goes in the garbage... which means I recycle and compost like there's no tomorrow. I try to avoid plastic wherever possible and I buy in bulk whenever I can.





Check out my zero waste blog here: https://zerowastesuperhero.wordpress.com/



Low Carbon

I bike, hike, and bus pretty much everywhere. My carbon footprint is tiny and at the end of the year, I'll make a donation so that I'm carbon neutral.

Local Organic Food

I make a point to always buy organic and local whenever I can. I also cook regularly and avoid processed food.

Sustainable Brands

I do as much as I can to research things that I buy and where they come from. I'm super into farmers markets, bees wax wrap, and recycled toilet paper (super glamorous I know).
